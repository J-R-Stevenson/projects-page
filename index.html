Finished Projects

  • Recent Trends in Treasuries
    In this post, I take a look at some recent trends in the U.S. Treasury market: SOMA holdings, ownership, auctions, liquidity, term premia, and so on. The ~50 charts were created in Excel, which is also where the bulk of the data processing for this project was done (with a few lines from Python and VBA included).
  • Binomial tree visualizer and calculator
    • I use Python to create a web app consisting of two parts. The first is a binomial tree calculator that prices a variety of financial options according to user-selected parameters. The second is an essay on the theory of binomial trees.
  • ARIMA modeling of 30 year mortgage rate
    • In this project, I walk through the steps of constructing an ARIMA model for the 30 year mortgage and use it for forecasting.
